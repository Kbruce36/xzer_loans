How to Apply

Getting started with Xzer is simple and secure. Our streamlined application process ensures your personal information is protected while we gather everything needed to process your loan quickly.

Application Requirements:
  • Full name exactly as shown on National ID
  • Clear photos/scans of National ID (front and back)
  • Complete contact information (phone and email)
  • Full address details (street, city, district, postal code)
  • Monthly income information
  • Loan amount and purpose details
Application Process:
  1. Submit Application: Complete our secure online form with all required information and upload your ID documents
  2. Admin Review: Our experienced team reviews your application within 24-48 hours
  3. Approval Call: If approved, we'll call you to schedule an office appointment
  4. Fund Collection: Visit our office with your original National ID to complete verification and collect your funds
  5. Final Verification: Quick in-person ID verification ensures security for both parties
Important Notes:
  • All uploaded documents must be clear and readable
  • Information must match exactly with your National ID
  • You must bring your original National ID for fund collection
  • Funds are disbursed in cash at our secure office location
