F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility requirements for a loan?

To qualify for a loan with Xzer, you must be at least 18 years old, have a steady income, valid identification, and meet our credit requirements. Specific requirements may vary based on the loan type and amount.

How quickly can I get my loan approved?

Most of our loans are approved within 24 hours. For urgent needs, we offer same-day approval for qualified applicants. Once approved, funds are typically disbursed within 1-2 business days.

What interest rates do you offer?

Our interest rates start from as low as 5.99% APR and vary based on factors such as credit score, loan amount, loan term, and income. We strive to offer competitive rates in the market.

Can I pay off my loan early without penalties?

Yes! We offer flexible repayment options with no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan early to save on interest costs, or make extra payments to reduce your loan term.

What documents do I need to apply?

Basic documents include government-issued ID, proof of income (pay stubs or tax returns), bank statements, and proof of address. Additional documents may be required based on the loan type.

Do you offer loans for people with bad credit?

Yes, we work with borrowers across all credit spectrums. While credit score affects loan terms, we consider multiple factors including income and employment history to make lending decisions.
